Is this just another AI image generator?

Not even close. AI image generators give you random outputs from random prompts. Claw Brand AI is a Brand Team Operating System - it saves your brand identity, manages your assets, uses structured templates, and produces consistent, on-brand mockups every time. It's the difference between a slot machine and a production studio.

Do I need any design experience?

Zero. If you can upload a file and click a button, you can use Claw Brand AI. The entire workflow is three steps: upload your brand, pick a template, click generate. Built specifically for non-designers who need professional results.

What kinds of mockups can I create?

166 templates across 12 categories: digital devices (phones, laptops, tablets, monitors), apparel (t-shirts, hoodies, caps), books and journals, product packaging, signage and displays, stationery, food and beverage, events, print assets, home and lifestyle, and 48 premium pro-scene lifestyle mockups. Plus unlimited custom scenes with SceneScaper AI.

How is this different from Canva?

Canva is a template editor - you drag elements around a flat canvas. Claw Brand AI uses agentic AI to generate photorealistic 3D mockups from your brand assets. It also saves your brand kit, manages your design assets, maintains a mockup library, and includes agency tools. They're not the same category of product.

1200px for web-optimized exports (perfect for sales pages, social media, and digital ads) and 3000px for print-quality exports (perfect for packaging, signage, and physical materials). Both formats from the same generation - no upscaling tools needed.
